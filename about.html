Building Excellence Since 2014

PicklePro was founded by sports enthusiasts and civil engineers who saw the need for professional, high-quality pickleball courts.

What started as a small family business has grown into a nationwide leader in court construction and resurfacing. We pride ourselves on using the best materials and latest technology to deliver surfaces that players love.
