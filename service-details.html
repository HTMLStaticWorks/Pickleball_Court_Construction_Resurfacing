Court Resurfacing Details

Precision-engineered acrylic coating systems designed for professional performance and long-lasting durability.

Why Professional Resurfacing Matters

Over time, UV exposure, environmental factors, and intense gameplay can degrade your court's surface. This results in inconsistent ball bounce, reduced player traction, and structural vulnerability. Our multi-layer resurfacing system doesn't just fix the aesthetics; it restores the professional playability of your court.

UV Protection

Our coatings feature advanced pigments that resist fading even in extreme sun exposure.

Moisture Barrier

Integrated sealants prevent water penetration and sub-surface damage.

Our Rigorous 5-Step Process

1
Deep Surface Cleansing

High-pressure industrial washing to remove contaminants and ensure perfect adhesion.

2
Structural Remediation

Precision injection of specialized crack fillers and leveling of low spots.

3
Advanced Primer Application

A dedicated bonding agent to lock the new surface to the existing base.
