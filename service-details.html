Court Resurfacing Details

Premium acrylic coating systems for peak performance.

Why Resurface Your Pickleball Court?

Over time, UV rays, rain, and heavy foot traffic can wear down the acrylic surface of your court. This leads to slippery spots, uneven ball bounce, and unsightly cracks. Our resurfacing process restores the texture, color, and integrity of your court.

Our Multi-Layer Process:

  • Pressure Washing: Removing all debris and stains.
  • Crack Repair: Filling and sealing any structural issues.
  • Primer Coat: Ensuring maximum adhesion for the new surface.
  • Acrylic Color Coats: Two layers of UV-resistant sports coating.
  • Official Line Marking: Precision painting for professional play.
